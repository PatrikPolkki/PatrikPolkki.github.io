About me

I am Patrik, 21 years old student at Metropolia University of Applied Sciences. As a major, I study mobile development, but I can also code web pages. My main areas of expertise include Javascript, HTML, CSS, Node.js, Kotlin and React Native.
Creating new things made me excited about coding and learning a profession for myself from it. In coding, I am most fascinated by learning new things and getting better. Also, the possibilities of creation are limited to your own imagination, and that if nothing motivates.

I am also familiar with other coding languages, which we studied at school. In the first year, we coded Arduino Zumobot to follow a line and fight against other robots in C language. We also did a Mobile health application via Android Studio, with Java and in the game development we did a first-person fighting game, with Unity and C#.
In my second year studying mobile development as a major, I have made a word quiz app called Language Quiz in Kotlin language via Android studio. We also made social media website called Wildlife Collection, which includes a responsive design with HTML and CSS, and back-end REST API with Node.js.

Projects

Wildlife Collection

Wildlife Collection is a social media for people who like to spend time in nature and with animals.
With Wildlife Collection you can share your photos or videos and also the location where it was taken.
Wildlife Collection also allows you to like and comment others media.
If you do not want to register as a user, you can browse others media without liking or commenting feature.

Language Quiz

Language Quiz is an app that helps in memorizing Finnish words. In this case, Language Quiz contains only words about animals.
Language Quiz asks ten questions in random order each time. when the Quiz is finished, the app will show your score, which you can also share with others.
Language Quiz also keeps a track of answers and corrects answers in the database, which you can see in the result section.
